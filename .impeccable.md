# .impeccable.md
Design context for Romain Pena's portfolio/business site.

---

## Design Context

### Users
Two audiences with equal weight:
- **Shopify brand founders** — evaluating whether to hire a specialist for a monthly retainer. They've likely been burned by generalist developers or bloated agencies. They're skeptical, time-poor, and want proof before they commit.
- **E-commerce agencies** — looking for a reliable Shopify specialist to subcontract to. They need confidence in technical depth and professionalism.

Both often arrive via referral or warm lead — they're already half-convinced. The site's job is **validation**, not persuasion.

**Job to be done:** Confirm that Romain is as good as they've heard. Build enough trust to reach out.

---

### Brand Personality
**Calm · Premium · Human**

Not a marketing page — a personal site. The voice is that of a trusted senior colleague: approachable but clearly expert, warm without being casual. No performance, no hype. Just quiet confidence backed by real work.

**Emotional goal:** "This guy clearly knows his stuff."
Visitors should leave feeling reassured, not sold to.

---

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Minimal and typography-driven** — whitespace and type carry the design; visual decoration is subordinate to content
- **Dual-mode**: both light and dark are first-class experiences
  - Light: off-white (`#FAFAF8`) background, near-black (`#111111`) text
  - Dark: deep navy (`#0D131F`), cyan (`hsl(187 80% 56%)`) as muted accent
- **Green/cyan accent sparingly** — Shopify green (`#96BF48`) light, cyan dark; used only for tags, borders, small hover states — never as a dominant color
- **Animations are purposeful, not decorative** — GSAP power2/power3 easing, stagger reveals, ScrollTrigger; motion communicates structure, not flash
- **Inter throughout** — single typeface, weight/size variation does all the heavy lifting
- No strong external references. The aesthetic is the work.

---

### Design Principles

1. **Credibility over persuasion** — Every design decision should reinforce trust. Show, don't sell. Case studies, specificity, and restraint all signal expertise more than claims do.

2. **Whitespace is voice** — Generous spacing signals that Romain is selective and deliberate. Crowded layouts would contradict the premium positioning.

3. **Typography does the work** — Hierarchy, weight contrast, and scale should communicate structure before color or decoration. The `.text-overline` + heading + body pattern is the primary layout unit.

4. **Calm interactions** — Hover states, transitions, and scroll animations should feel smooth and inevitable, never eager or flashy. Power2/power3 easing, subtle transforms, no bounce.

5. **Human, not corporate** — First person always. No jargon, no buzzwords, no aggressive CTAs. The site should feel like a conversation with someone competent, not a pitch deck.

## Token Reference

| Token | Light | Dark | Usage |
|-------|-------|------|-------|
| `--background` | `hsl(0 0% 100%)` → `#FAFAF8` | `hsl(225 15% 8%)` → `#0D131F` | Page background |
| `--foreground` | `hsl(0 0% 12%)` → `#1F1F1F` | `hsl(220 10% 92%)` | Body text |
| `--muted` | `hsl(142 50% 35%)` → Shopify green | `hsl(187 80% 56%)` → cyan | Accent — use sparingly |
| `--border` | `hsl(220 7% 85%)` | `hsl(225 10% 20%)` | Dividers, card edges |
| `--primary` | `hsl(220 14% 24%)` | `hsl(222 100% 97%)` | Headings, strong UI |
| `--secondary` | `hsl(220 5% 96%)` | `hsl(225 10% 16%)` | Subtle backgrounds |

## Animation Conventions

- **Entrance**: `power3.out`, duration 0.9–1.1s, from `y: 30` or `opacity: 0`
- **Scroll reveals**: `power2.out`, duration 0.7–0.8s, ScrollTrigger at 80–85% viewport
- **Stagger**: 0.12–0.15s between siblings
- **Hover**: color/border transitions only — no scale unless it's a card flip
- **Text animations**: Framer Motion for scramble/reveal; GSAP for character-level splits

## Market Research Findings

### Pricing — you're underpriced by ~20%

| Tier | Current | Recommended | Market benchmark |
|---|---|---|---|
| Growth | €900 / 12h (€75/hr) | €1,200 / 12h | Storetasker vetted devs ~$100/hr |
| Partner | €1,500 / 20h (€75/hr) | €2,000 / 20h | Boutique agency retainers $3,000–8,000/mo |

Senior Shopify specialist rate range in North America / W. Europe: **$75–$150/hr**.
Your client roster (Atari, TOGETHXR, Jayson Home) justifies the higher end.

### Competitive Landscape

Three tiers in the market:
- **Commodity** — "I know Liquid/HTML/CSS", menu of services, compete on price. $15–60/hr.
- **Specialist** — lead with specific outcome/expertise, show case studies. $75–120/hr. *This is where you are.*
- **Authority** — personal brand around a specific intersection, work comes via inbound/referral. $150–200+/hr. *This is where you're heading.*

Premium differentiators (what separates top 10% from the rest):
1. Named recognizable clients (you have this — Atari, TOGETHXR, Jayson Home)
2. Specificity of service (you don't fully exploit this yet)
3. "Custom code not app installs" signal (huge buyer pain point)
4. Communication reliability (48h response, retainer model addresses this)

### Your Biggest Positioning Gap

**"Figma-to-Shopify specialist"** is a real search term with genuine buyer intent.
No strong independent developer ranks for it — only agencies (Arctic Grey etc.).
You do this work. You don't lead with it loud enough.

### Market Gaps You Can Own

1. **Figma-to-Shopify as a named specialty** — underserved by independent devs
2. **Design agency subcontracting slot** — agencies that produce Figma files but have no reliable Shopify dev partner (highest LTV opportunity)
3. **Custom app development for mid-market brands** — very few devs can do both Liquid + backend apps
4. **OS2.0 migration specialist** — Shopify deprecating legacy apps Jan 2026, urgent demand through 2026
5. **Gaming/entertainment vertical** — your Atari work is directly relevant, no visible Shopify specialist community serving it

### What Buyers Complain About (address in copy)

- "Anyone can call themselves a Shopify developer" → show named clients, specific case studies
- "They installed 6 apps instead of writing 30 lines of Liquid" → explicitly signal custom code approach
- "They disappeared after launch" → retainer model, response time, Slack
- "Communication was terrible" → monthly calls, explicit response commitments

## Acquisition Channels (Priority Order)

1. **Referrals** — highest close rate, best LTV. Ask every retainer client.
2. **Design agency outreach** — 50 targeted LinkedIn DMs to non-Shopify design/UX agencies in Montreal + major US cities. One relationship = 5–10 projects/year. Highest leverage per hour.
3. **LinkedIn** — consistent posting on Shopify-specific topics (problems solved, techniques, client results). Even low-engagement posts generate DM conversations.
4. **Shopify Partner Directory** — passive discovery, Shopify implied endorsement. Tag: Custom Liquid, App Development, Klaviyo Integration.
5. **Storetasker** — apply for listing. Sets $100/hr minimum expectation.
6. **Twitter/X** — slower, but builds relationships with agencies and other devs who refer overflow work.

### Directories to list on

- Shopify Partner Directory (primary)
- Storetasker (apply — vetted)
- Clutch.co (agencies use it for vetting)
- Contra (freelancer-first, Shopify category)
- EcomRolodex (Shopify-specific)
